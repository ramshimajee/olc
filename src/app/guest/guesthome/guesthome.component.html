Join OLC: Empowering Ideas, Fostering Discovery

Open Library Consortium (OLC) is a vibrant platform designed for researchers, students, and academics to publish and explore research ideas. By subscribing to OLC, members gain access to a wealth of resources, expert guidance, and interactive events like seminars, workshops, and conferences. OLC supports researchers at every stage, helping them refine their ideas, connect with peers, and stay updated on research trends. Subscribe now to join a community dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation!

2024 – 2025 Board Members

Open Library Consortium (OLC) Board oversees the governance, organizational structure, by-laws, and the review and direction of the membership of the LPC. This group provides guidance on critical decisions, formalizes documentation, and ensures that projects make progress towards their goals. The Board is elected by LPC’s membership, and officers are elected by the Board.
